Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

At Dairy Muchh, we strive to provide the freshest and highest quality dairy products. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review our refund policy below.

1. Eligibility for Refund

- Refunds are only applicable for products that are *damaged, expired, or compromised* upon delivery.

- The product will be eligible for return within *12 hours* after delivery.

- Requests for refunds must be made within *24 hours* of delivery.

- Perishable items such as milk, curd, and buttermilk are *non-refundable* unless they are defective upon arrival.

2. Non-Refundable Items

- Products that have been *opened, consumed, or improperly stored* after delivery.

- Orders placed incorrectly by the customer.

- Any product purchased during *special promotions, discounts, or clearance sales* is non-refundable.

3. Refund Process

- To request a refund, please *contact our customer support* with the following details:

  • Order ID
  • Reason for refund request
  • Photos of the product (if damaged or expired)

- Once your request is verified, refunds will be credited within *5-7 business days*.

4. Replacement Policy

- Instead of a refund, we may offer a *replacement product* if available.

- The replacement order will be delivered within *24 hours*.

5. Cancellation Policy

- Orders can be canceled within *1 hour* of placing the order for a full refund.

- Once an order is processed or out for delivery, it *cannot be canceled*.
