Know Your Milk

Milk is one of the most nutritious and essential food items consumed worldwide. It is a rich source of calcium, protein, and vitamins that contribute to bone health and overall well-being. However, not all milk is the same. Understanding the different types of milk and their benefits can help you make the right choice for your health.

Types of Milk

There are various types of milk available in the market, each catering to different dietary needs:

  • Whole Milk: Contains full fat, making it ideal for children and those needing extra energy.
  • Skimmed Milk: Low in fat but rich in protein and calcium, suitable for weight-conscious individuals.
  • Organic Milk: Comes from cows raised without synthetic hormones or antibiotics.
  • Raw Milk: Unprocessed and packed with natural nutrients, but requires proper handling.
  • Plant-based Milk: Alternatives like almond, soy, and oat milk for lactose-intolerant individuals.
