Our products include Milk, Cheese, Paneer, Ghee and Yogurt. DAIRY MUCHH dairy products are derived from milk, which has been an important source of nutrition for people.

HISTORY OF OUR MILK

DAIRY MUCHH Milk is the primary source of nutrition for young mammals (including breastfed human infants) before they are able to digest solid food. Our milk contains many nutrients, including calcium and protein, as well as lactose and saturated fat.

More than twenty eight million people in Telangana consume DAIRY MUCHH milk and milk products.

HISTORY OF DAIRY MUCHH DAIRY

DAIRY MUCHH Dairy got its start in what is now TELANGANA in about 2022 , the first milk from animals was turned into yogurt, cheese, and butter. DAIRY MUCHH has the TELANGANA'S largest dairy, producing over 30 million liters of milk everyday. DAIRY MUCHH is first among all DAIRIES in both production and selling of milk. Most of the milk is domestically consumed.
