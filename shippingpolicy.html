Shipping Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

At Dairy Muchh, we are committed to providing fast and reliable delivery of fresh dairy and farm products to our customers.

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently deliver DairyMuchh farm products across select cities in India. Please enter your pin code at checkout to verify delivery availability.

2. Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within **2 hours** of confirmation.
  • Orders placed before 5 PM IST are typically processed and scheduled for delivery the next morning.

3. Delivery Time

  • All orders will be delivered **the very next morning** after order placement.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email. Additionally, you can track your order in real-time through the **Dairy Muchh mobile application**.

5. Delivery Attempts & Failed Delivery

  • Our delivery team will make two attempts to successfully deliver your order.
  • If delivery is unsuccessful after two attempts, the order will be marked as undeliverable, and a refund will be processed.
  • No additional shipping charges will be applied for re-attempted deliveries.

6. Address & Contact Information

Customers must ensure that the shipping details provided at checkout are accurate and up-to-date. DairyMuchh will not be responsible for delays or failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses.

7. Perishable & Farm Fresh Products

  • Dairy and farm-fresh products should be refrigerated immediately upon delivery.
  • If you receive a damaged or expired product, contact customer support within **12 hours** of delivery with photographic proof.
