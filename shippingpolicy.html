1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship DairyMuchh farm products across select cities in India. Please enter your pin code at checkout to check delivery availability.

2. Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation.

  • Orders placed after this time will be shipped the next day.

3. Delivery Time

  • Standard Delivery: 2-5 business days, depending on location.
  • Express Delivery (if available): 1-2 business days.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can track your order in real-time.

5. Delivery Attempts & Failed Delivery

  • Our courier partner will make two delivery attempts.
  • If undelivered, the package will be returned, and a refund (excluding shipping charges) will be processed.

6. Address & Contact Information

Ensure correct shipping details to avoid delays. DairyMuchh is not responsible for delivery failures due to incorrect addresses.

7. Perishable & Farm Fresh Products

  • Dairy and fresh farm products require immediate refrigeration upon delivery.
  • If you receive a damaged or expired product, contact support within 12 hours of delivery with photos.
