Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

At Dairy Muchh, we are committed to protecting your privacy across our web and mobile e-commerce platforms. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website or mobile app:

  • Personal details (name, phone number, email, delivery address).
  • Payment details (securely processed via third-party payment gateways).
  • Order history and preferences to enhance user experience.
  • Device and browsing data to optimize performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process orders and facilitate seamless delivery.
  • Enhance user experience through personalized recommendations.
  • Provide customer support and service updates.
  • Ensure transaction security and fraud prevention.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. However, we advise users to safeguard their account credentials.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share information with:

  • Delivery and logistics partners to fulfill orders.
  • Payment providers for transaction processing.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and analytics tools to enhance website functionality and user experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Update or delete your information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on our website with an updated effective date.
